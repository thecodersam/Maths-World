About Me

Hello! I am Samuel Godswill. You might be surprised to hear that I am only 10 YEARS OLD. Yes, you read that right. So, how did I learn this algebra and calculus? Well, I have to give credit to my family, who pushed me to be ahead of what I'm taught in schools.
